Waste Management Facts

Recycling one ton of paper can save 17 trees.

Plastic bottles take 450 years to decompose in a landfill.

The average person generates over 4 pounds of trash every day.

Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) programs hold manufacturers accountable for the end-of-life disposal of their products, encouraging sustainable practices..

Biodegradable materials may not break down efficiently in landfills, where conditions may not be conducive to rapid decomposition.

Recycling aluminum saves up to 95% of the energy required to produce the same amount from raw materials.

Incineration of waste materials can release dioxins, which are highly toxic and harmful to human health.
